Client Case: Daymaker Touring

Frontend consultation to drive sales

Daymaker Touring needed updates to their product page and required minor changes to site design. This provided me with an opportunity to ajust the flow of the project page and to include a call-to-action for each section.

This client hosted on Shopify and gave me the opportunity to work with this content management system. The Shopify template language is Liquid which exposed me to a new kind of development environment that I had to quickly learn. No charges were handed over to the client for this chance to learn the new technology.

Since this client is local I was able to meet with them in person and establish trust in the work. We reviewed their goals and what would be the best way to accomplish them. Finally, I set to work with milestones that were delivered to the client after completion.
